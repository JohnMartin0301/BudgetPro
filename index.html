BudgetPro

BudgetPro is designed to help you manage your finances efficiently. Using the 50/30/20 rule, it allows you to allocate your monthly allowance into three categories: Needs (50%), Savings (30%), and Wants (20%). You can enter, edit, or delete transactions in these categories, and the app will automatically update your balance and notify you if you exceed the budget in any category. The app also provides a visual summary of your spending using charts, saves your data locally for later access, and allows you to print a detailed report, including the chart, for any specified month.
